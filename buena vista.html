Buena Vista

Buena Vista is located in the Upper Arkansas River Valley, adjacent to the Sawatch Range Mountains which contains some of Colorado's highest peaks. If you know Spanish, you know that Buena Vista is translated "good view." Additionally, the Arkansas River is Colorado's #1 rafted river so sign up for a white water rafting excursion.
