Salida

Salida sits right at 7,000 feet above sea level, near the Continental Divide. The county has more mountain peaks soaring to 14,000 ft. that any county in Colorado, often helming it the "Fourteener" Region. Salida is situated in a valley with 330 days of annual sunshine and a climate similar to Tucson, AZ.
